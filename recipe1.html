Eggplant Parmesan Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Peel and slice the eggplants. Dip each slice in beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan. Fry eggplant slices until golden brown.
  4. In a baking dish, layer marinara sauce, fried eggplant, Parmesan, and mozzarella. Repeat.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
