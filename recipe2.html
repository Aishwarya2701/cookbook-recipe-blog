Lemon Ricotta Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: In a bowl, mix ricotta, flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Step 2: Add eggs, buttermilk, and lemon zest. Mix until just combined.
  3. Step 3: Heat a griddle or pan over medium heat. Spoon batter onto the griddle.
  4. Step 4: Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
