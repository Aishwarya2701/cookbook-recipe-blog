Chilli Crunch Tofu Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons chili flakes
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Press tofu to remove excess water, then cube it.
  2. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Coat tofu cubes in the mixture.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a pan. Fry tofu until golden brown. Set aside.
  4. In the same pan, saut√© garlic, ginger, and chili flakes. Add brown sugar and stir until caramelized.
  5. Return tofu to the pan, coating it in the chili mixture.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with chopped green onions.
