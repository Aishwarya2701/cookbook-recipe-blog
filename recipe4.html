Raspberry & White Chocolate Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 200g digestive biscuits
  • 100g melted butter
  • 600g cream cheese
  • 200g white chocolate, melted
  • 150g fresh raspberries
  • 100g sugar

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Crush the digestive biscuits and mix with melted butter. Press into the base of a springform tin.
  2. Step 2: Beat cream cheese until smooth, then add melted white chocolate and mix well.
  3. Step 3: Gently fold in raspberries and sugar into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Step 4: Pour the mixture onto the biscuit base and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.
